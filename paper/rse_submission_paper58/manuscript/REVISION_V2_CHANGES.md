## Manuscript-side (rewrites, tables, and figures)

### Headline changes

1. **Title** — from "Scale-Adaptive Geospatial Foundation-Model Dynamics for Baseline Land-Surface Change Prediction" → "Frozen Geospatial Foundation-Model Embeddings as a Change-Signal Screening Layer: What They Can and Cannot Do, with a Scale-Adaptive Allocation Ablation".
2. **Naming** — the single "SA-GeoFMD" umbrella label is retired. The dynamics component is now **GeoFM-LDN**; the allocation post-processor is **SA-Alloc**. Section 4.1 (Architecture overview) explicitly explains why the two are kept separate.
3. **Abstract** — rewritten to lead with the paired-inference verdict, not the aggregate mean. Explicitly reports the 0.20 end-year accuracy gap vs persistence and the mixed per-area FLUS wins.
4. **Contributions** — reduced from 7 to 5 items, framed around what the paper _tests_ and _rules out_ under rigorous statistics.

### Section-level changes

- **§5.2 Aggregate results** — now includes `tab:paired_inference` with Wilcoxon, paired-t, permutation, sign test, Cohen's d, and required-n-for-80%-power (=65). Val-area exclusion sensitivity is reported.
- **§5.6 Independent categorical change validation** — Wuyi 2020–2021 (0% ref change) explicitly excluded from means; new `tab:independent_change_paired` reports paired Wilcoxon on 11 pairs against 3 controls on 5 metrics.
- **§5.7 Same-grid GeoSOS-FLUS ablation** — renamed and rewritten. Old aggregate-mean table demoted to `tab:geosos_flus_same_grid_legacy`; new `tab:flus_per_area_wins` shows per-area win/loss for 5 variants across 4 metrics with sign-test and Wilcoxon p-values. Prior "5/5 seed wins" narrative is retracted.
- **§5.8 Multi-step degradation** — all 10 valid cached areas now listed (7 with 1-step only; v1 reported only 3).
- **§5.9 Cosine similarity gap does not track end-year accuracy** — new subsection with `tab:cosine_to_accuracy`.
- **§5.5 Change-pixel analysis** renamed to "Self-consistent embedding-displacement diagnostic" with explicit note that it is a co-linearity check, not an independent test (S2).
- **§4.3 Scenario placeholder** — expanded and anchored (`sec:limitations_scenario`); Figure 1 caption now marks scenario as dashed / reserved.
- **§4.6 LULC decoder** — added ESRI 9→6 class merge rule and decoder time-stability caveat.
- **§4.7 Scale-adaptive allocation (SA-Alloc)** — renamed, and now enumerates all 11 hand-tuned constants with a scope note pointing to limitation `lim:magic_numbers`.

## What is NOT in this revision

The following reviewer requests would have required experiments we could not run
in-scope, and are honestly listed as future work in Limitations §7.7:

- Expanded validation to 30–50 cached areas (M1 second-half): out of GEE budget.
- Native-driver GeoSOS-FLUS deployment (M3 second-half): no locally calibrated ANN drivers for our AlphaEarth/ESRI regions.
- Prithvi patch/token-level ablation (M4): no cached patch-level Prithvi outputs for the 17 areas.
- Terrain-context ablation (S4): the ablation experiment was never run.
- Per-year decoder retraining (S6): future work.
- Splitting off §6 (Planning) into its own paper (M5): kept but reduced and reframed.
